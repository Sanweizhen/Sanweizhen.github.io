Endangered Species

Collaborative project /with @Beyond Words Studio

While the concept of endangered animals is well known, other species at risk are often overlooked. This project aims to show the disparity in the assessment of animals and fungi on The IUCN Red List, with the goal of raising awareness about the importance of protecting endangered fungi.

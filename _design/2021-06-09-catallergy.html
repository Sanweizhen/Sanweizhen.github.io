Allergy to Cats

This project is a bittersweet experience! I love cats but am also allergic to them, which can be quite distressing. Unfortunately, I am a true cat lover. I believe there are many people who share my struggles and feelings, which is why I created a brochure filled with cute illustrations that have a touch of interactivity. The brochure reflects the complex and conflicting relationship I have with my cat and I hope that other cat lovers and sufferers will relate to it.

Women Entrepreneurs

Collaborative project

This collaborative project with UN Women aims to address the survey data from the Women's Entrepreneurship EXPO 2022 in two ways.

Output1: EXPO report

Output2: Colour Your Chanllenges




↓

Process


In Output1 we have divided the report into four parts, in the first part we show the importance of women entrepreneurs, in the second part we show the challenges and obstacles they encounter, in the third part we introduce some existing policies and in the fourth part we call for action.



For Output2, we used the frequency data from the Women's Entrepreneurship EXPO on the question "Main challenges in getting the business started" to design an poster.

We have selected watercolours similar to those used in the UN Women Thematic Area Colour scheme.
Just as women entrepreneurs encounter a variety of challenges that are not single and divisive, but often interact and mix, so the colours used to represent these difficulties flow and mix with each other in the water.
We would like to invite women entrepreneurs to show their difficulties with marble painting.

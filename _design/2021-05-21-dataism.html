Live With Data

When I discovered that data was becoming one of the most compelling criteria by which we judge things, I began to consider the impact of data on the human spirit and life.

In this project, I wanted to help people reflect on the role of data for people through an immersive exhibition. To do this, I designed a series of posters, a guided tour app, fonts, and merchandise.

I developed a navigation app for exhibitions to enhance the visitor experience, however, this app also collects data on their actions and preferences within the exhibition hall, potentially impacting their evaluation of exhibits. This demonstrates the growing impact of quantifiable evaluations in modern society, which can no longer be disregarded and are capable of shaping thoughts and decisions.

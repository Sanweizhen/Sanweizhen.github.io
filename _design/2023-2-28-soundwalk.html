Step By Step: A Walk to Nowhere

Collaborative project

Every day, we are accompanied by the sound of footsteps, yet we often fail to notice its presence. In this project, we experimented to explore the visualisation of sound, connecting the sound of footsteps with visual changes in graphics and color.

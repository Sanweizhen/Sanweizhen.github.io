Withme Chocolate

The goal of this packaging design for chocolates is to bring joy to those who may be feeling down or lonely. The outer packaging opens to reveal six chocolates with illustrations that are sure to bring a smile to your face, each in a different flavor. These chocolates are specifically aimed at city dwellers who might feel empty and sad, whether due to being away from loved ones or just feeling lonely.

The natural sweetness of chocolate releases feel-good chemicals like dopamine and serotonin, helping to lift one's mood. When feeling blue, a small indulgence in these chocolates can serve as a pick-me-up. In designing the packaging, warm and bright colors were chosen to draw the eye and create a sense of comfort and positivity for the customer.
